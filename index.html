About Us

We are a family-run team that understands how important it is for Americans to keep their leather furniture and car interiors in perfect condition. Leather is not just a material — it’s comfort, style, and an investment in long-term quality of life. We understand this because we value quality ourselves and strive for perfect results in every project. Our journey began with a desire to help people preserve the beauty and durability of leather items. We received professional training in the technologies of the British company Letech, one of the world’s leaders in leather care and restoration. We are certified and use only proven and safe products and methods in our work. Our mission is to give your leather a second life. Whether it’s a sofa, armchair, or car interior — we approach every job with care, precision, and respect for detail. We understand how much American families value cleanliness and the condition of their homes and vehicles. We are ready to become your trusted partners in leather care. Reach out — and see for yourself!
